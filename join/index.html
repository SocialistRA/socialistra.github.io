Become a Member

Attention all single mothers, people of color, trans individuals, leftists, and working class people everywhere:

Is the typical gun culture not your scene? Are you new to shooting and looking to get started somewhere? Does the political atmosphere of the NRA and associated gun clubs turn you off? Do the gun ranges in your town make you feel uncomfortable? Perhaps the SRA is right for you!

If you are any of the following: Working class, anarchists, eco-warriors, class warriors, animal liberators, anti-fascists, anti-racists, anti-capitalists, ultra left-wing radicals, LGBTQ+ – YOU are all welcome to join!

Benefits of SRA Membership:

  • Gain access to monthly range meetings with other members
  • Ammo is provided at monthly range meetings for members by the local chapter
  • Access to other events
  • Gain access to SRA training, including curriculum and instruction
  • A judgment-free environment with like-minded individuals

The local chapter organizers will keep your membership information on file for purposes of keeping in contact. You will be issued a membership card and SRA patch upon your first payment of dues. Submit your membership application to your local chapter in order to be inducted into the organization.

Head on over to the Contact Us page for information regarding your local chapter.


The Socialist Rifle Association's mission is to arm and train the working class for self-defense. We are working class anarchists and communists — anti-fascist, anti-racist, anti-capitalist radicals.
